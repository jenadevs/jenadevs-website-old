Events

Our Meetups take place monthly.

  • March 2016

    The Softwerkskammer Jena is Born

    Several chats with the founders and maintainers of the [Agora platform](http://github.com/softwerkskammer/Agora) were going into details while the initial connection has been established for several years. As a result a new local group was created for Jena at [softwerkskammer.org](http://www.softwerkskammer.org/groups/jena) and the twitter channel.

  • April 2016

    MEETUP #001: Docker Party

    We visited the Docker Meetup in Berlin for the [3rd Docker Birthday](https://www.docker.com/community/docker-birthday-3) in Berlin upfront and brought that Party a week later to Jena. For this evening we even enhanced the single workshop character with a talk beforehand and three more workshops of our own. Our event got featured on the official Docker blog and a box with Docker shirts and different stickers was send to us from California. Unfortuntately persistent negotiations with the German custom offices delayed the delivery.
